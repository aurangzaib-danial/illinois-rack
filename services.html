Anodizing & Plating Racks

Illinois Rack engineers understand plating processes and design each rack to maximize part capacity, allow easy loading and unloading, and meet all processing requirements without compromising the quality of the finish.

This drive for efficiency ensures that Illinois Rack customers purchase the minimum number of racks possible to accomplish their plating needs. This translates into direct cost savings.

With our six different tanks varying in size from three feet deep to eleven feet deep and an oven large enough to cure the largest rack, size is rarely a limitation.

Paint Racks

At Illinois Rack, we understand the need to produce consistent paint quality at the lowest possible cost.

Each rack, whether for dip, electrostatic flow or powder coating or spray, is designed to ensure quality paint application and optimum utilization.

Thorough knowledge of painting processes enable our engineers to develop the best methods of securing parts without diminishing paint quality.

We can also provide maintenance (cleaning) of all sizes of paint and powder coating racks and hooks.

Printed Circuit Racks

The continually changing needs of the printed circuit industry require the ability to quickly design and manufacture specialized equipment.

Illinois Rack has the experience necessary to rapidly respond to such demands.

Whether your application requires a rack, fixture or basket, Illinois Rack can produce the appropriate configuration to satisfy your printed circuit processing requirements.

Plastisol Coating

Illinois Rack offers several types of plastisol coating in many colors for rust and corrosion protection, cushioning, color-coding and aesthetics.

Baskets

Illinois Rack provides a wide variety of basket designs to meet your anodizing, degreasing, dipping, drying, heat treating, plating and material handling requirements.

In addition to our extensive inventory of standard designs, Illinois Rack can custom design baskets out of steel, titanium, aluminum and stainless steel in bar, rod, mesh, expanded or perforated configurations to meet your individual specifications.

Plastic Fabrication

We manufacture plastic recovery baskets to specification for plating tanks and plastic parts of many kinds for the plating industry.

Fixture Fabrication & Repair

Illinois Rack has a full service welding shop ready to handle your repair requests.

We can repair or rebuild your existing fixtures to extend their useful life. This saves you money by reducing the need to purchase replacement fixtures.

Our technicians are experienced in mig and spot welding and are ready to provide rapid turn around.

Carts

Illinois Rack can design and manufacture specialized racks for storage and/or transport of a large variety of finished parts.

Tanks & Ovens

Our tanks and ovens can accommodate items up to 11' high and 2.5' x 4' wide.
